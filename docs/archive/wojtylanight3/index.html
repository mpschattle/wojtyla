    Wojtyla Night 3: Feast of St. Benedict #

    Hosted by Alex Lohn and Paul Davies at Benedict’s Bungalow (their house).

    1. Amazing Grace + Blackbird by the Beatles, Paul Davies
    2. Reading from the Desert Fathers + I Have Made Mistakes by The Oh Hellos (with added original verse), Alex Lohn
    3. Reading from the Rule of St. Benedict + Sweet Home Alabama by Lynyrd Skynyrd, Rhett Jones
    4. Fiddle Tunes, Rae Corrigan
    5. “Conway’s Game of Life”, a Computer Simulation, Nate Meyers
    6. Holy Sonnets VI-X by John Donne, Michael Schattle
    7. Poetry Reading, Becca Kee
    8. “Make You Feel My Love” by Bob Dylan, Madeleine Stoll
    9. “Tortoise” (Original Painting), Julian Richardson
    10. Excerpts from “The Ballad of the White Horse” by G.K. Chesterton, Garrett McMillan
    11. “Church Street Blues” by Tony Rice, Louis Grace
