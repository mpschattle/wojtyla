    Wojtyla Night 2: Incarnation #

    Hosted by Alex Lohn at his Apartment. The theme of this event was “Incarnation”.

    1. “Only at Christmas Time” by Sufjan Stevens, Alex Lohn
    2. Marian Hymn, Dennis Hodapp
    3. “Boil Them Cabbage Down” and other fiddle tunes, Rae Corrigan
    4. Painting: “Annunciation” by Beatrice Emma Parsons, Garrett McMillan
    5. “Manifesto: The Mad Farmer Liberation Front” by Wendell Berry, Adrienne Prochaska
    6. From a homily by St. John Chrysostom: Office of Readings, First Friday of Lent, Johnny Knoebel
    7. Original Songs, Mariana Barron Esper
